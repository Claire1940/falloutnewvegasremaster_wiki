<Section icon={Shield} title="Hit Feedback and Ragdoll Physics">

In the vanilla game, enemies rarely show physical reactions to being shot, outside of stiff, pre-baked stagger animations. This lack of feedback makes weapons feel weak. Introducing physics-based ragdolls and locational force resolves this issue entirely.

Mad A's Ragdolls Mod is essential for establishing realistic death reactions and hit momentum. When an NPC is shot, their body reacts dynamically based on the caliber of the weapon used and the specific limb that was struck.

| Mod Name | System | Core Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Mad A's Ragdolls | Physics | Locational force and realistic death momentum |
| Dramatic Staggering | Animation | Smooth, physics-aligned stagger reactions |
| Dismember Tweaks | Mechanics | Limits limb dismemberment to high-caliber weapons |
| Custom Hit Markers | User Interface | Audio cues for body, head, and critical hits |

To prevent cartoonish violence, Dismember Tweaks restricts limb loss to high-powered weapons. Small-caliber pistols will no longer cleanly sever limbs, making high-caliber rifles and shotguns feel uniquely devastating.

<Callout type="success" title="Feedback Success">
Combining locational ragdoll physics with custom hit marker sounds provides perfect combat feedback. You will immediately know if you landed a headshot at long range without needing to check the enemy's health bar.

<Section icon={Target} title="Gameplay Mechanics Overhaul">

To tie your visual, audio, and animation mods together, you must overhaul the underlying combat mechanics. The base game relies heavily on the VATS system to compensate for poor real-time shooting mechanics. By introducing modern recoil, ballistics, and slow-motion targeting, you can make real-time gunplay engaging and skill-based.

Installing Immersive Recoil 2 adds physical recoil patterns to every weapon, requiring active mouse control to stay on target. This works in tandem with Physics-Based Ballistics, which adds bullet drop and travel velocity to all projectiles.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Install Just Assorted Mods (JAM)">
    This framework replaces the static VATS system with a dynamic, real-time Bullet Time mechanic. It drains your Action Points (AP) while slowing down time, allowing for precise, manual aiming during chaotic encounters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Immersive Recoil 2">
    Open the in-game Mod Configuration Menu (MCM) and adjust the recoil recovery speed to match your personal preference. Ensure 'Crosshair Return' is enabled for a smoother transition back to center.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Apply Realistic Movement Fixes">
    Activate Realistic Movement to eliminate the erratic, instant dodging behavior of hostile NPCs. This ensures enemies move at believable speeds, making manual aiming fair and rewarding.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Gameplay Tip">
To assist with aiming on high-resolution monitors, enable the Soft Lock Aim Assist option. This subtle feature helps guide your crosshair to targets when aiming close to their hitboxes, smoothing out the engine's natural aiming jank.
</Callout>
