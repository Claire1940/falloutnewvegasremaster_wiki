After setting your S.P.E.C.I.A.L. attributes, you will undergo a series of tutorials with Sunny Smiles to learn the basics of combat, crafting, and survival in the Mojave Wasteland.

<Section icon={Target} title="The Road South: Primm and Nipton">

After leaving Goodsprings, follow the road south toward Primm. The town is currently overrun by escaped convicts from the NCR Correctional Facility (NCRCF). You must rescue Deputy Beagle from the Bison Steve Hotel to gain information on the men who shot you. This section introduces the game's first major choice: who should lead Primm?

| Candidate | Requirement | Outcome for Primm |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primm Slim** | 35 Science or 3 Fission Batteries | Independent town, mild robotic protection |
| **NCR (Sgt. McGee)** | Request reinforcements from Mojave Outpost | NCR Annexation, high taxes, high security |
| **Meyers** | Secure a pardon from NCR or use Speech | Frontier justice, moderate security, independent |

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazardous Route">
Avoid heading directly north from Goodsprings toward New Vegas. The area is infested with **Deathclaws** and **Cazadores** that will easily kill low-level players. Always follow the southern loop through Primm and Novac.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Rescue Deputy Beagle">
    Enter the Bison Steve Hotel in Primm. Use stealth or brute force to clear the convicts and untie Beagle in the kitchen area.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit Mojave Outpost">
    Head further south to the Mojave Outpost to speak with Major Knight. This is a great spot to repair gear and pick up the 'Cold, Cold Heart' quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Nipton Massacre">
    Enter Nipton to witness the aftermath of Caesar's Legion's "lottery." Speak with Vulpes Inculta to receive your first formal introduction to the Legion's ideology.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Navigating the Factions: NCR, Legion, and House">

As you approach the New Vegas Strip, the political landscape becomes more complex. You will be forced to interact with the three major powers vying for control of Hoover Dam. Your **Fallout New Vegas walkthrough** must account for your reputation with these groups, as being "Vilified" will result in hit squads being sent after you.

<Callout type="info" title="The Mark of Caesar">
Upon leaving the Tops Casino after confronting Benny, you will be approached by a Legion messenger. He will grant you the **Mark of Caesar**, which resets all negative reputation with the Legion. Use this opportunity to visit The Fort even if you plan on siding with the NCR.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="The Battle for Hoover Dam">

The climax of the game takes place at Hoover Dam. Regardless of your chosen path, you will need to secure the support of minor factions to ensure victory. These include the Boomers at Nellis Air Force Base, the Brotherhood of Steel in Hidden Valley, and the Great Khans in Red Rock Canyon.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Boomers' Support">
    Reach Nellis Air Force Base by dodging artillery. Complete tasks for Mother Pearl and help Loyal raise the B-29 bomber from Lake Mead.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Brotherhood Conflict">
    Locate the Hidden Valley bunker. You can either broker a truce between the NCR and the Brotherhood (requires Elder McNamara) or destroy the bunker entirely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Final Assault">
    Speak to your faction leader to trigger the endgame. You will fight across the dam to either kill or talk down the opposing commander (General Oliver or Legate Lanius).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Point of No Return">
Before starting the final quest (e.g., 'Eureka!', 'No Gods, No Masters'), the game will warn you that you cannot return to the open world. Ensure you have completed all DLCs and side quests before proceeding.
</Callout>

</Section>
