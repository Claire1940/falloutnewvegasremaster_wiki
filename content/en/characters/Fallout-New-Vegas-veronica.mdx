<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Quest Walkthrough">

Once you trigger three dialogue events, Veronica will request to return to the Hidden Valley Bunker to speak with Elder McNamara. This begins the core sequence of "I Could Make You Care."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Consult Elder McNamara">
    Travel to the Hidden Valley Bunker. With Veronica in your party, you bypass the security protocols. Speak with Elder McNamara on the second basement level. Veronica will attempt to argue for a change in the Brotherhood's isolationist codex, but the Elder will dismiss her concerns.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access Elijah's Terminal">
    Leave the bunker and head west toward an abandoned shack near Helios One. Access Father Elijah's hidden terminal inside to download his research notes. This reveals three potential technologies that could convince the Elder to reconsider his position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Retrieve the Pulse Gun">
    Acquire the key to the secure cabinet from Colonel Blackwell's quarters at Nellis Air Force Base. Travel to Vault 34, navigate the flooded corridors, and unlock the armory. Retrieve the prototype Pulse Gun from the locked gun case.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Present the Technology">
    Return to Hidden Valley and present the Pulse Gun to Elder McNamara. Despite the weapon's ability to easily disable power armor, the Elder refuses to alter the codex, leaving Veronica disillusioned.
  </Step>
</Steps>

At this point, you must guide Veronica through her life-defining choice: stay with her family in the Brotherhood of Steel, or leave the faction to build a new life with the Followers of the Apocalypse.

| Ending Choice | Perk Gained | Perk Effect | Story Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Stay with Brotherhood | Bonds of Steel | +4 Damage Threshold | Remains a Scribe; faces internal isolation |
| Leave Brotherhood | Causeless Rebel | +30% Unarmed Attack Rate | Followers outpost is destroyed; she wanders |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Paladin Ambush">
Regardless of your choice, rogue Brotherhood Paladins will confront you upon leaving the bunker or arriving at the Followers outpost. If Veronica leaves the faction, these Paladins will slaughter the innocent researchers at the outpost. Be prepared for a high-difficulty combat encounter.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking the Scribe Counter and Best Builds">

Beyond her main quest, you can unlock a secret combat technique from Veronica by appealing to her love for pre-war fashion. Veronica frequently mentions her desire for a beautiful, stylish dress to wear instead of her bulky scribe robes.

To unlock this hidden interaction, place a piece of pre-war formal wear into her inventory. Eligible items include the Formal Wear from the Ultra-Luxe, the Chiffon Dress from Vault 21, or Vera's Outfit from the Dead Money DLC. Upon receiving the dress, she will excitedly offer to teach you the Scribe Counter, a unique unarmed move that allows you to execute a quick, staggering counter-attack immediately after successfully blocking an enemy strike.

| Gear Slot | Recommended Item | Location / Source | Combat Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Weapon | Pushy | Ruby Hill Mine | High damage with a powerful knockback effect |
| Alternative Weapon | Ballistic Fist | Major weapon merchants | Massive single-hit damage on contact |
| Power Armor | T-51b Power Armor | Hidden Valley Safehouse | Elevates her DT to survive endgame encounters |
| Helmet | Scribe Hood | Default equipment | Retains her signature aesthetic |

<Callout type="success" title="Scribe Counter Requirement">
You do not need to complete her companion quest to unlock the Scribe Counter. You can perform this exchange immediately after recruitment by purchasing a dress from Sarah Weintraub in the Vault 21 gift shop.
</Callout>
