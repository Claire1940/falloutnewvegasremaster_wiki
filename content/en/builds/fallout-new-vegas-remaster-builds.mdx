### Key Attribute Breakdowns

- **Strength**: Determines your carrying capacity and weapon handling. Heavy weapons like the Anti-Materiel Rifle require 8 Strength, though this can be mitigated with the Weapon Handling perk.
- **Perception**: Required for long-range red compass markers and critical sniper perks like Sniper and Better Criticals (requires 6 Perception).
- **Endurance**: Governs your total health, environmental resistances, and the number of cybernetic implants your body can tolerate.
- **Charisma**: Primarily a dump stat for solo builds. However, it increases companion damage and armor, making it useful for squad-focused setups.
- **Intelligence**: Dictates how many skill points you receive per level up. Starting with at least 6 or 7 Intelligence ensures you can easily max out multiple skills.
- **Agility**: Directly impacts your Action Points for V.A.T.S. and speed of drawing and reloading weapons.
- **Luck**: Increases all skills slightly and directly boosts your critical hit chance. A Luck stat of 9 or 10 is essential for critical-oriented setups.

<Callout type="warning" title="Perk Requirements">
Do not lower your Perception below 6 if you plan on playing a sniper. The Better Criticals perk requires 6 Perception and 6 Luck, and it is arguably the most important damage perk in the game.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Character Progression">

To get the most out of your character, follow this systematic leveling and progression path. This ensures you acquire key perks and equipment at the optimal times.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation and Traits">
    Allocate your SPECIAL points at the Doc Mitchell screen. Select traits like **Built to Destroy** for a flat critical chance bonus, or **Good Natured** to boost your non-combat utility skills at the start of your journey.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Tag Skills and Early Quests">
    Tag your primary combat skill (Guns, Energy Weapons, or Melee) along with Lockpick and Sneak. Complete the early Goodsprings quests to gather basic supplies, leather armor, and starting ammunition.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Vegas Medical Clinic Run">
    Head toward the New Vegas Medical Clinic as early as possible. Avoid combat by sneaking past the deathclaws via the northern passage, and save your caps to purchase the Intelligence and Agility implants from Usanagi.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mid-Game Perk Synergy">
    As you reach levels 12 to 20, select cornerstone perks like **Shotgun Surgeon** or **Silent Running**. Focus on upgrading your weapons at the Gun Runners kiosk outside Freeside.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Leveling Optimization">
Take the Skilled trait at character creation for a flat +5 to all skills. The 10% experience penalty is negligible, as there is more than enough content in the game and its DLCs to reach the level cap easily.
</Callout>
