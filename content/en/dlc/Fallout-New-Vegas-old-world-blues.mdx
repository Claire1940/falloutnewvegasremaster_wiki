### Tactical Breakdown of Top-Tier Weapons

*   **Christine's COS Silencer Rifle**: This is arguably the best ballistic sniper rifle in the game. It trumps standard sniper rifles in almost every category, featuring a built-in suppressor and a massive critical multiplier. It allows you to clear out entire camps of Lobotomites silently from long range.
*   **Elijah's Jury-Rigged Tesla Cannon**: Located at Elijah's Watch, this weapon boasts zero weapon spread, a feature other Tesla Cannons cannot claim. It deals massive electrical damage, making it highly effective against the robotic defenses of Big MT.
*   **Elijah's Advanced LAER**: This weapon is compatible with standard LAER mods, such as the Prismatic Lens. Instead of splitting the beam into three, it maintains a single, highly concentrated beam that deals devastating damage.

<Callout type="success" title="Editor's Choice">
Always prioritize retrieving **Christine's COS Silencer Rifle** as soon as you enter the mid-game phase of the DLC. Its low AP cost of 38 and high critical multiplier make it a staple for both stealth and open-combat builds.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Defensive Gear and Unique Armor Sets">

Surviving the hazards of Big Mountain requires more than just high damage output; you also need robust defensive gear. The expansion features several unique armor sets that offer exceptional utility, skill boosts, and unique active effects.

### Key Armor Highlights

*   **Christine's COS Recon Armor**: Found right next to the central Auto-Doc in the Y-7 Medical Facility, this armor provides a solid 19 DT and enhances your sneak capability. It is a darker, sleeker variant of the standard recon armor.
*   **Hazmat Suit & Cowl**: Found in the Hazmat Testing Ground northwest of X-8. It does not require a passcode if you have the EM Pulse upgrade for your Sonic Emitter. It offers an incredible 85% poison resistance, which is perfect for dealing with the toxic hazards of the crater.
*   **Atomic Valence Tri-Oscillator**: To obtain this headgear, you must test-fire the artillery cannon at the X-7A Left Field Launch pad. This opens up a crater in X-7B Boomtown. Inside a Foot Locker within that crater, you will find this halo-like device, which grants passive health regeneration.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hardcore Mode Warning">
The **Atomic Valence Tri-Oscillator** is highly recommended for Hardcore players. The passive health regeneration mitigates constant chip damage, saving you valuable food and healing resources during long exploration runs.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Upgrading the Stealth Suit Mk II">

The **Stealth Suit Mk II** is one of the most famous items in the **Fallout New Vegas old world blues** DLC. Not only does it possess an artificial intelligence personality that talks to the player, but it can also be systematically upgraded by completing research trials.

Follow these steps to locate, secure, and fully upgrade your stealth armor to its maximum potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Retrieve the Base Armor Components">
    Head to the **X-13 Research Facility**. Inside, bypass the energy barriers using your Sonic Emitter. Collect the gloves, boots, and chest piece from the storage room to assemble the base Stealth Suit Mk II.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initiate the Infiltration Tests">
    Access the terminal inside the X-13 testing area to begin the basic stealth simulation tests. Avoid the robot guards and retrieve the research documents from the safes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Advanced Firmware Trials">
    Complete all consecutive test runs. Each successful simulation run rewards you with a firmware upgrade, permanently enhancing the suit's automated systems.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Ultimate Stealth Upgrades">
    Upon completing all tests, your suit will be fully upgraded. It will now provide **+25 Sneak**, **+1 Perception**, **+1 Agility**, and a **20% increase to crouched movement speed**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Armor Classification Note">
Note that the Stealth Suit Mk II is classified as **Medium Armor**. Consequently, light armor perks like *Light Touch* or *Travel Light* will not apply while wearing this suit.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Sonic Emitter Frequencies & Scientist Gloves">

The Sonic Emitter is a highly versatile weapon given to you by the Think Tank at the start of the DLC. By finding hidden holotapes throughout Big MT, you can unlock five distinct frequencies, each offering unique critical hit effects. Additionally, you can find specialized Scientist Gloves that deal elemental or stat-reducing effects.

### Sonic Emitter Frequencies

| Frequency | Critical Hit Effect | Location |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revelation** | Paralyzes targets for 10 seconds | Starting weapon from Think Tank |
| **Gabe's Bark** | Creates a powerful knockback effect | X8 Research Center (Kennels) |
| **Opera Singer** | Dismembers the target instantly | Higgs Village (House 108) |
| **Tarantula** | Sets enemies on fire; chance to explode | Higgs Village (House 00) |
| **Robo-Scorpion** | Deals 100 explosive damage | Forbidden Zone Dome |
