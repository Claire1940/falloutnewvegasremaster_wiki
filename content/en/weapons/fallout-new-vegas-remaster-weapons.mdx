The WAP (Weapon Animation Replacer / Weapon Enhancement Pack) Year One Pack by Anon X is the premier choice for ballistic overhauls. This mod replaces the models and textures for 30 distinct weapons, including the iconic Hunting Rifle, the 10mm SMG, and the heavy-duty Anti-Materiel Rifle. Because these changes are implemented through scripts rather than standard ESP files, they do not occupy a plugin slot, keeping your game highly stable.

For players who prefer alternative designs, the AWO (Alternative Weapon Overhaul) series offers high-quality replacements for weapons like the 10mm Pistol and the Hunting Shotgun. If you choose to combine WAP and AWO mods, you must hide the conflicting WAP scripts for those specific weapons inside your mod manager (such as Mod Organizer 2) to ensure the correct models load in-game.

<Callout type="tip" title="ESPless Modding Advantage">
Always prioritize ESPless mods when overhauling weapon visuals. Because they run entirely on scripts, they bypass the engine's hard plugin limit, allowing you to run hundreds of visual improvements simultaneously.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Step-by-Step Installation & Compatibility Setup">

Setting up a heavily modded weapon loadout in New Vegas requires a precise installation order to prevent crashes, missing textures, or broken animations. Follow this structured setup guide to establish a stable foundation for your remastered arsenal.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Install Core Script Extenders">
    Before installing any weapon mods, you must install the foundational script extenders. Download and install **xNVSE**, **JIP LN NVSE Plugin**, **Johnny Guitar NVSE**, **kNVSE**, and **ISControl Enabler** through your mod manager. These utilities allow the modern scripts and custom animations to run smoothly in the game engine.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Weapon Model Overhauls">
    Install your chosen visual replacers, starting with the **WAP Year One Pack**. If you want to use alternative models from the **AWO series** (such as the AWO 10mm Pistol), install them immediately after. Open your mod manager, locate the WAP folder, and hide or delete the specific scripts for any weapons you want the AWO mod to override.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install Classic Fallout Weapons Remastered">
    Download and install the main **Classic Fallout Weapons Remastered** mod. Ensure you also download any optional configuration files. If you are playing a Tale of Two Wastelands (TTW) playthrough, the mod will automatically detect your setup and apply the necessary integration patches without requiring extra files.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Apply Animation Patches and Sound Overhauls">
    Install animation sets like **Hitman's Seasons** to give your new weapon models realistic handling, reloading, and firing movements. Finally, install **All Weapon Sounds Overhaul Modern Edition (AWSOME)** to replace the audio files. Run your load order sorting tool (LOOT) to finalize your active plugins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Conflict Resolution Required">
If you experience invisible weapons, red exclamation marks, or rapid game crashes upon drawing a weapon, it means you have conflicting animation sets or missing requirements. Double-check that kNVSE and ISControl Enabler are fully updated and active in your mod manager.
</Callout>

</Section>
