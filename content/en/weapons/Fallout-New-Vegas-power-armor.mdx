<Section icon={Shield} title="Unlocking Fallout New Vegas Power Armor Training">

Power armor represents the ultimate defensive gear in the Mojave Wasteland, offering unparalleled Damage Threshold (DT) and unique stat boosts. However, unlike earlier titles, you cannot simply pick up a suit and wear it immediately. You must first secure specialized training from one of the few factions that still retain pre-war military knowledge. 

There are two primary factions capable of teaching you how to operate this heavy machinery: the Brotherhood of Steel and the Enclave Remnants. Each path involves a distinct set of quests, faction alignments, and moral choices.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Brotherhood Route**: Requires completing a series of fetch quests for Elder McNamara in Hidden Valley.
- **Enclave Route**: Triggered through Arcade Gannon's companion quest "For Auld Lang Syne" near the end of the game.
- **Alternative Option**: Learn how to utilize NCR Salvaged Power Armor without training.

<Section icon={Map} title="The Brotherhood of Steel Questline">

The Brotherhood of Steel is the most straightforward faction to approach for training. They reside in the Hidden Valley bunker complex, located east of Sloan. The area is surrounded by sandstorms and bark scorpions, making navigation tricky. Look for the bunker door marked with "ban the bomb" graffiti to find the correct entrance.

If you enter without Veronica, the Brotherhood will strip you of your gear and place an explosive collar on your neck. You will be forced to deal with an NCR Ranger stationed in a nearby bunker. Once cleared, or if you brought Veronica along, you will gain access to Elder McNamara and begin the lengthy quest "Still in the Dark."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find the Missing Patrols">
    Elder McNamara will task you with locating three missing Brotherhood patrols. You must retrieve holotapes from dead paladins located at the REPCONN Headquarters, near the Black Mountain path, and close to Camp Forlorn Hope.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Gather Scout Reports">
    After returning the tapes, you must travel across the Mojave to gather field reports from three hidden Brotherhood scouts. These scouts are positioned near major faction hubs, including Camp McCarran, HELIOS One, and the Nevada Highway Patrol Station.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Salvage Vault Components">
    The bunker's air filtration system is failing. You must venture into three dangerous vaults to recover replacement parts: Vault 11 (air filter cartridges), Vault 3 (pulse generator), and Vault 22 (diffuser cartridges). Be prepared to fight spore carriers, fiends, and security robots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Install the Black Mountain Transmitter">
    The final task requires you to head to the mutant-infested peak of Black Mountain. Sneak or fight your way to the second floor of the radio building, unlock the terminal, and install the remote monitoring device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Upon completing these tasks, speak to Elder McNamara. He will officially induct you into the Brotherhood of Steel, grant you the Power Armor Training perk, and gift you a refurbished suit of T-45d Power Armor.

| Vault Location | Primary Threat | Required Item | Recommended Level |
|-|-|-|-|
| Vault 3 | Fiends / Motor-Runner | Pulse Generator | 10+ |
| Vault 11 | Security Turrets / Sentry Bots | Diffuser Cartridges | 15+ |
| Vault 22 | Spore Carriers / Mantises | Air Filter Cartridges | 8+ |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Hardin Alternative">
During the quest, Paladin Hardin will ask you to help him overthrow Elder McNamara. Siding with Hardin is possible, but it delays your training. Hardin will only grant training after you complete his personal questline once he becomes Elder.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="The Enclave Remnants Questline">

For players looking for the absolute best armor in the game, the Enclave Remnants route is highly recommended. This route is unlocked via Arcade Gannon, a companion found at the Old Mormon Fort in Freeside. To trigger his personal quest, "For Auld Lang Syne," you must earn his trust and progress far enough into the main storyline.

To earn Arcade's trust, you need to accumulate "history points" by traveling to locations that trigger dialogue about his past or the Enclave. Helpful locations include the crashed Vertibird, HELIOS One, and the Silver Rush in Freeside. Choose dialogue options that favor independent Mojave progression or support the Followers of the Apocalypse.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Recruit Arcade Gannon">
    Visit the Old Mormon Fort. You can recruit Arcade by having a high Speech skill, using the Confirmed Bachelor perk, or having a high reputation with the Followers of the Apocalypse.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Progress the Main Quest">
    You must advance the main story to the point where the battle for Hoover Dam is imminent. This means progressing Yes Man's, Mr. House's, or the NCR's questlines to their final stages. Note that Arcade will not trigger this quest if you side fully with Caesar's Legion.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruit the Five Remnants">
    Arcade will ask you to locate and recruit five former Enclave members scattered across the desert. You must visit each individual and convince them to meet at the Remnants Bunker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resolve the Bunker Dispute">
    Once all five are gathered at the Remnants Bunker, you must decide whether they should support the NCR or Caesar's Legion at Hoover Dam. If you choose the NCR, you must pacify Orion Moreno with a high Speech check or defeat him in combat.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resolving the political dispute in the bunker, Judah Kreger or Arcade Gannon will provide you with the Power Armor Training perk. Additionally, you will receive Daisy Whitman's old suit of Remnants Power Armor, or the highly coveted Gannon Family Tesla Armor.

| Remnant Member | Wasteland Location | Recruitment Condition |
|-|-|-|
| Daisy Whitman | Novac | Ask about her history as a Vertibird pilot |
| Cannibal Johnson | Cannibal Johnson's Cave | Respect his isolated lifestyle |
| Judah Kreger | Westside | Convince him to fight one last battle |
| Orion Moreno | Moreno Agricultural Ranch | Remind him of his past duty |
| Dr. Henry | Jacobstown | Complete the quest "Guess Who I Saw Today" first |

<Callout type="tip" title="Siding with the Legion">
If you wish to side with Caesar's Legion but still want Enclave training, you must trigger Arcade's quest before making your final, irreversible alliance with Caesar. Consult the [Official Fallout Wiki](https://fallout.wiki/) for advanced sequence-breaking methods.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Best Power Armor Suits and Stats">

Once you have secured training, you will want to seek out the best possible protection. The Mojave Wasteland contains several distinct models of power armor, each tailored to different playstyles and character builds.

The Remnants Power Armor offers the absolute highest Damage Threshold in the base game, making you virtually unkillable. However, it is incredibly heavy and degrades quickly. For energy weapon builds, the Gannon Family Tesla Armor is the superior choice, offering a massive boost to energy weapon skills while weighing significantly less.

If you do not want to go through the lengthy questlines to get training, the NCR Salvaged Power Armor is a decent stopgap. The NCR stripped the joint motors and microfusion packs from captured Brotherhood armor, turning them into heavy metal suits that do not require training. However, this stripping process removed all stat bonuses and increased the overall weight.
