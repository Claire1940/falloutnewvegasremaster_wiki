<Section icon={Target} title="Side Quests and Bounty Hunting Guides">

Side quests in the Mojave offer some of the most entertaining writing and unique combat scenarios in the game. From hunting down deranged fiends to recruiting specialized staff for Freeside casinos, these missions showcase the dark humor and grit of the wasteland.

### Three Card Bounty

Assigned by Major Dhatri at Camp McCarran, this quest tasks you with hunting down three notorious Fiend leaders. To secure the maximum reward, you must kill them without damaging their heads, which requires precise targeting in VATS or melee combat.

| Target | Location | Combat Style | Key Drop |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Cook-Cook | South Vegas Ruins | Flamethrower, Heavy Armor | Cook-Cook's Ingredients |
| Violet | Violet's Camp | Sniper, Guard Dogs | Violet's Brain |
| Driver Nephi | Samson Rock Crushing | Melee (Golf Club) | Nephi's Golf Driver |

### Wang Dang Doodle

James Garrett at the Atomic Wrangler casino in Freeside wants to expand his business by hiring three unique entertainers. This quest is highly valued for its comedic writing and low combat requirements, making it an excellent early-game source of caps.

### Birds of a Feather

Working for the Van Graff family at the Silver Rush in Freeside provides access to premium energy weapons. However, this questline presents a major moral dilemma, as it eventually requires you to betray your companion, Rose of Sharon Cassidy (Cass), to the Van Graffs.

| Quest Step | Objective | Key Requirement | Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Guard Duty | Guard the Silver Rush door | Identify peaceful vs hostile NPCs | 150 Caps |
| Weapon Deal | Escort Gloria to a deal | Keep the buyer calm | 200 Caps |
| The Betrayal | Bring Cass to Jean-Baptiste | Choose to sacrifice Cass or fight | Plasma Rifle, Caps |

<Callout type="warning" title="Permanent Companion Loss">
If you choose to complete the Van Graff questline by sacrificing Cass, she will be permanently removed from your game. This also fails her personal companion quest, "Heartache by the Numbers."
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Companion and Faction Questlines">

Companion quests offer deep insights into the lore of the Mojave, particularly regarding the struggles of legacy factions like the Brotherhood of Steel and the Enclave.

### Veronica's Dilemma: I Could Make You Care

Veronica Santangelo's personal quest highlights the internal struggle of the Brotherhood of Steel. She seeks ancient technology to convince the Elder that the Brotherhood must adapt to survive. You must travel across the wastes to find a farming technology, a pulse gun, or a biological weapon research data. Ultimately, regardless of the technology presented, the Brotherhood remains stubborn, forcing Veronica to decide whether to stay or leave the order.

### Arcade Ganon's Heritage: For Auld Lang Syne

Arcade Ganon's quest allows you to reunite the surviving members of the Enclave to assist in the final Battle of Hoover Dam. This quest is highly sought after because it rewards you with the best power armor in the game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gain Arcade's Trust">
    Travel to key historical sites with Arcade in your party, such as the Repconn HQ, the Crashed Vertibird, and the Silver Rush, to trigger his dialogue prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Progress the Main Story">
    Advance the main questline until you are close to the Battle of Hoover Dam. Arcade will then approach you about recruiting his old family friends.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruit the Enclave Remnants">
    Visit the five retired Enclave members scattered across the Mojave and convince them to attend a reunion meeting at the Remnants Bunker.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Decide Their Allegiance">
    At the bunker, choose whether the remnants should fight for the NCR or Caesar's Legion. This choice determines the final gear you receive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Power Armor Training">
Completing Arcade's quest is one of the few ways to obtain Power Armor Training without joining the Brotherhood of Steel, allowing you to equip the Gannon Family Tesla Armor immediately.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Main Story Showdowns and High-Stakes Intrigue">

The main questline of the game features incredible player freedom, allowing you to decide the fate of the entire region through diplomatic manipulation or brute force.

### Beyond the Beef

This quest is widely considered one of the most complex roleplaying missions in modern gaming. You are tasked with investigating the disappearance of Ted Gunderson at the Ultra-Luxe casino, which leads to discovering a secret sect of the White Glove Society attempting to return to cannibalism.

| Path | Key Actions Required | Reputation Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Expose Mortimer | Find a replacement meal, expose Mortimer during his speech | Positive White Glove reputation |
| Assist the Cannibals | Frame Ted's father or sacrifice a companion | Negative Karma, White Glove alliance |
| Violent Rescue | Fight your way through the casino to save Ted | Hostile White Glove Society |

### Arizona Killer

For players aligned with Caesar's Legion, this quest tasks you with assassinating NCR President Aaron Kimball during his visit to Hoover Dam. The mission provides unparalleled freedom in how you execute the target.

| Assassination Method | Tools Required | Detection Risk |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Sniper Shot | Sniper Rifle, High Sneak | Low (if positioned well) |
| Bombing the Vertibird | Repair 50 or Science 50 | Medium (requires disguise) |
| C4 Helmet Trick | C4, Sneak, Pickpocket | Low |
| Anti-Aircraft Gun | Repair 50 | Medium |

### No Gods, No Masters

The ultimate climax for players choosing the Independent Vegas path with Yes Man. You must secure Hoover Dam, repel both the Legion and the NCR, and decide the final fate of the region's leadership.
