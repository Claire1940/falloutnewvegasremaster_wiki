<Section icon={Shield} title="Technical Specifications and Engine Upgrades">

The **Fallout New Vegas remaster leak** suggests a significant technical leap. According to internal reports, the remaster will not use the aging Gamebryo engine from 2010. Instead, it is being rebuilt within the Creation Engine 3, the same technology platform currently powering *The Elder Scrolls VI* and the early pre-production of *Fallout 5*.

This shift allows for several critical improvements that were impossible on the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360:
- **Zero Loading Screens**: The transition between the Mojave Wasteland and the New Vegas Strip is expected to be seamless.
- **Enhanced AI**: NPCs will utilize the upgraded "Radiant AI" system seen in more recent Bethesda titles.
- **Modern Lighting**: Full support for Ray Tracing and advanced volumetric lighting to capture the harsh desert sun.
- **Stable Modding**: A more robust framework for the modding community, which has kept the original game alive for 16 years.
