<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Engine Comparison: Creation vs. Unreal Engine 5">

One of the most debated topics within the community is whether the **fallout new vegas remaster engine** should stick to Bethesda's proprietary tools or move to Unreal Engine 5. The original game was built on the Gamebryo engine, which, while flexible for modders, is notoriously unstable on modern hardware. A move to Unreal Engine 5 would offer unparalleled lighting and asset streaming via Nanite and Lumen, but it poses a significant risk to the existing modding ecosystem.
